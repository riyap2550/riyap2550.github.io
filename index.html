Education is a human right

Give Girls An Education

Many girls worldwide do not receive the education that they deserve. It is important that every girl receives an education no matter what circumstances they are in. Costs, transportation, and bias towards boys are some reasons as to why girls are not provided with a chance to learn. With the help from everyone, we can end this issue and give girls a book and a pencil to learn whatever they desire.
